Living in Subang Jaya

An area guide for buyers settling into Subang

Subang Jaya pairs the energy of a mature township with the calm of older, tree-lined neighbourhoods. The Federal Highway and LDP put Kuala Lumpur, Petaling Jaya and the airport corridor within easy reach, while the LRT line keeps daily commutes simple.

Families come for the schools and the parks; professionals for the connectivity. Across USJ, SS-districts and the Shah Alam border, Subang properties hold their value because the fundamentals — access, amenities and greenery — rarely go out of style.
